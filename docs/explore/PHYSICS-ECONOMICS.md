# PHYSICS-ECONOMICS: Conservation Laws and Market Mechanisms

## Premise

Physics teaches that the deepest properties of a system are its conserved quantities. Economics shows how markets coordinate distributed actors without central planning. Both inform how Synapse thinks about invariance, evolution, and governance.

---

## Physics

### Conservation Laws

Every symmetry corresponds to a conserved quantity (Noether's theorem).

**Synapse:** Content-addressing conserves identity. Hash function is the "symmetry."

### Thermodynamics

- **Entropy:** Systems tend toward disorder
- **Free energy:** Systems prefer low-energy states
- **Second law:** Entropy increases

**Synapse:** Vault entropy increases monotonically (append-only). Information accumulates.

### Phase Transitions

Qualitative change at critical thresholds.

**Synapse:** Governance threshold is a phase transition. Below: disorder. Above: ordered promotion.

### Quantum Mechanics

- **Superposition:** Multiple states before observation
- **Collapse:** Measurement creates definite state
- **Entanglement:** Correlated systems

**Synapse:**
- Candidate blobs exist in superposition of "proposed"
- Governance collapses to "promoted" or "rejected"
- Telemetry entangles execution with outcome

---

## Economics

### Market Mechanisms

Prices coordinate distributed actors:
- Signals scarcity
- Allocates resources
- Provides incentives

**Synapse:** Execution routing as market. Cost × Latency × Trust.

### Mechanism Design

Given desired outcomes, design rules that produce them from selfish actors.

**Synapse:** Governance as mechanism design. Fitness criteria as incentive structure.

### Commons (Ostrom)

Governing shared resources sustainably:
1. Clear boundaries
2. Proportional benefits
3. Collective choice
4. Monitoring
5. Graduated sanctions
6. Dispute resolution

**Synapse:** Vault as commons. Governance enforces Ostrom principles.

### Auction Theory

Allocating scarce resources:
- English (ascending)
- Dutch (descending)
- Sealed-bid
- Vickrey (truthful)

**Synapse:** Could governance slots be auctioned?

## Key Insights

### The Fitness Function is Economic

f(Link) = (SuccessRate × Integrity) / (Latency × ComputeCost)

This is:
- A market signal (resource allocation)
- A selection pressure (evolution)
- A phase transition criterion (physics)
- A Bayesian update (cognition)

**The same form, four interpretations.**

### The Vault is a Commons

Shared, governed, growing. Economics of commons apply:
- Externalities (every invocation has costs)
- Free-rider problem (benefit without contribution)
- Governance as solution

### Conservation of Identity

Content-addressing = identity is conserved. Like energy in physics.

If hash symmetry breaks (collision), identity conservation fails.
