# Principle: Protocols as Verifiable Experiments

## Definition
State is transient, truth is historical: Governance rules are not immutable laws; they are snapshots of our current understanding. They are evolved through verifiable experimentation, not static mandate.

## The One-Way Door Illusion
The "One-Way Door" is a design illusion: We do not choose permanent paths; we choose paths of inquiry. If a path encounters an anomaly, we do not patch it—we move the state machine to a new expression that accounts for the anomaly.

## The Cost of Erasure
The Cost of Erasure is Zero: We measure the progress of our fabric by the number of failed hypotheses we have successfully discarded, not by the amount of code we have accumulated. Our goal is a minimalist, self-correcting engine that discards its own dead weight.
